# Face Cam VR Icon Directions

These are concept directions for the next SVG app icon. They intentionally avoid headset, literal monitor, and padlock-first symbolism.

## 01 Focus L

Turns the `L` into a viewing aperture. This is the most brandable direction because it can become a standalone mark instead of a descriptive diagram.

Selected and refined as `option-01-refined.svg` / `public/icon.svg`. The refined version keeps the bold `L`, centered focus point, and soft spatial arc while simplifying the earlier sketch details.

## 02 Locked Gaze

An abstract gaze/target shape. It carries face-aware centering without drawing an actual camera.

Refined as `option-02-refined.svg` / `public/icon.svg` for comparison with the selected 01 direction. This version keeps the eye-like gaze silhouette and center focus while staying minimal.

## 03 Spatial Fold

Suggests VR-format space being flattened into a clean 2D path. More expressive, less literal.

## 04 Viewport

A simple eye-shaped viewport. This is the most readable at small favicon sizes, but it is also the most generic.

## 05 Lockless L

A stronger letterform mark with a stabilizing cross shape. It keeps the name in the icon without using text.

## 06 Look Arc

The simplest brand mark direction: `L`, focus point, and curved projection arc. Recommended starting point if the icon should feel less like a utility and more like a product.
